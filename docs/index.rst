Welcome to TRIDENT's documentation!
====================================

TRIDENT is a toolkit for large-scale whole-slide image processing, developed at the Mahmood Lab at Harvard Medical School and Brigham and Women's Hospital. 

.. image:: _static/trident_crop.jpg
   :align: right
   :width: 200px

Explore the full pipeline, from tissue segmentation to slide-level feature extraction, all backed by state-of-the-art foundation models.
